About me

I am 26 years old studying Networked Systems in the School of Computing at the University of Utah. I plan to graduate with my MS in the spring of 2020. My plans for after graduation will be to find a job as a software engineer. I enjoy programming in Java and C++ and feel really comfortable programming in them. I also know some Python, C#, and React Native. I enjoy Networked Systems and the logic behind how to get multiple servers talking to each other and be fault tolerant.

Over the summer I had the opportunity to work for FamilySearch as a backend Software Engineer Intern. It is an amazing place to work. I love the atmosphere that they provide their employees. They are very flexible attend are willing to work with your schedules. The team that I work on is a very helpful team and they have taught me so much since I started working there in May 2018. Since then I have been hired as a Student Software Engineer while I go to school for my Master's Degree.

I graduated from Utah State University in May 2018 with my Bachelor's in Computer Science with an emphasis in Software Developement and a minor in Spanish. While I was doing my undergrad, I took a Distributed Systems class, which was the class I enjoyed the most. I was able to work on a semester assignment where I programmed a distributed budget collaborating system. It allowed multiple clients to talk to multiple servers and log into specific budgets. Even allowing for multiple clients to log into the same budget. Users were also able to talking to each other through public and private chats.
