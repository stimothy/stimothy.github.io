About me

I am 25 years old studying Computer Science at Utah State University. I will be graduating in May 2018 with my bachelors degree in Computer Science and and Emphasis in Software Developement. I will also graduate with a minor in Spanish. My plans for after graduation is to get a job and attend a grad school studying Computer Networking.

I am from Syracuse Utah where I lived with my family of 6. I have 1 older brother and 2 younger sisters. I graduated from Syracuse High School in 2010 and work for a year waiting until I was old enough to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. In 2011 I left to the Dominican Republic and served as a missionary for two years teaching people about the gospel. I learned to interact and speak fluently to people in Spanish.

Half way through my schooling at Utah State I fell in love with a wonderful woman and in July 2015 we got married.
